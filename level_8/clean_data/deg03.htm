The details of minimum points scores given are for general information
only and do not form part of any contract.
They are not intended for use in determining whether any individual applicant
is or is not entitled to an offer of a higher education place.

FINAL  =  the lowest points score achieved by an applicant offered a place on this course.
MEDIAN =  the points score of the applicant mid-way between the highest and the lowest

*     Not all on this points score were offered places
**    Matriculated candidates are considered but admission is
      on the basis of performance in the music test and interview
***   Applicants are ranked as for other courses but the final
      decision depends on performance in interview
#     Test/Interview/Portfolio
AQA   All qualified applicants
+     Includes Project/Portfolio score
